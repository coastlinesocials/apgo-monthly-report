Executive Overview

September was a steady month for Apollo Silver's communications, with consistent social media activity and solid investor outreach through email campaigns. LinkedIn continued to generate the highest quality engagement, with technical updates such as the Calico MRE post driving impressions and click-throughs well above average. Twitter showed improvement in overall impressions and clicks, though engagement rates dipped slightly, pointing to the opportunity for more varied content formats.

Email campaigns performed well overall, with average open rates of 26 percent, comfortably above the industry benchmark of approximately 21 percent. The consolidation effective date announcement on September 10 was the strongest performer, confirming that milestone-driven communications are most effective. The month's results suggest that Apollo's audience continues to respond most strongly to project-specific and milestone-related updates.

The September 10 consolidation effective date email was the strongest performer of the month. Campaigns tied to specific milestones outperformed general updates, and unsubscribes remain low and within a healthy range. Recommendations & Next Steps

Strategic priorities for October 2025 and beyond

Social Media

Content & Cadence

  • Continue prioritizing project milestone posts with strong visuals including maps, graphics, and technical highlights.
  • Experiment with infographic or short video content to lift engagement on Twitter, including interview clips and California State coverage.
  • Increase LinkedIn activity slightly to 5 to 6 posts to capitalize on strong click-through interest.
  • Actively cross-promote third-party interviews or coverage featuring leadership by retweeting to reinforce Apollo's visibility.

Mailouts

Reach & Engagement

  • Re-send high-value news releases for Calico, financings, and permits to non-openers after 48 hours to capture additional reach.
  • Review the proposed social media post bank and begin posting slightly more frequently, targeting one non-news post per week plus any news or blog content.
  • Continue monitoring unsubscribe trends to ensure list health remains positive.
