Executive Overview

Apollo Silver's communications activity in January reflected a clear shift from visibility maintenance to measurable audience growth and expanded digital reach. Across social media, email, and website channels, engagement quality remained strong while reach and inbound activity improved materially, driven by financing announcements, leadership commentary, and heightened investor interest following the completion of the $27.5 million private placement.

Overall, January results demonstrate that recent communications activity translated into tangible growth across Apollo's digital channels. With improved visibility following the financing close and cleaner measurement now in place through GA4, the Company enters 2026 with a stronger and more measurable digital foundation to support ongoing investor outreach and engagement.

LinkedIn performance improved in January despite a limited posting cadence, with impressions, clicks, and follower growth all increasing month over month. Recommendations & Next Steps

Strategic priorities for February 2026 and beyond

Social Media

Grow & Sustain Visibility

  • Continue prioritizing milestone-driven updates including financings, project advancements, and leadership commentary, which consistently generate the strongest engagement.
  • Increase use of visual content and third-party coverage to support visibility and reinforce credibility around key announcements.
  • Monitor monthly performance to ensure continued growth in impressions, engagement, and website referral traffic.

Mailouts

Optimize & Retain

  • Maintain timely, news-driven mailouts, as open rates continue to outperform industry benchmarks.
  • Refine subject lines and calls to action to further improve click-through performance, particularly for longer-form content and leadership commentary.
  • Continue monitoring unsubscribe trends to ensure list growth and engagement remain healthy.

Website

Build on the Baseline

  • Use January as the new baseline for measuring website performance and audience growth going forward.
  • Prioritize SEO improvements across project and investor pages to support continued growth in Organic Search traffic.
  • Improve internal linking and homepage visibility for key updates to better direct visitors toward core project and investor content.

Ongoing

Reporting & Measurement

  • Maintain this monthly reporting framework to track performance and identify trends across all communications channels.
  • Continue strengthening internal tracking and measurement systems to support clearer evaluation of channel effectiveness and ROI.
