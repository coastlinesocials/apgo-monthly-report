Executive Overview

Apollo Silver's communications activity in October centered on advancing visibility around the Company's successful private placement financing and continued progress at the Calico Silver Project. The month featured consistent multi-channel engagement, highlighted by strong social media performance, exceptional mailout open rates, and the introduction of new website analytics tracking to better capture and understand audience behavior going forward.

Financing-focused posts, particularly those announcing the C$26.78 million placement closing, drove the highest engagement, confirming that capital and milestone-based news continues to resonate most with Apollo's audience. Mailout performance remained a major strength, with open rates averaging above 45 percent across six campaigns, nearly double the industry benchmark for mining issuers. Overall, October reflected continued consistency and healthy investor interest, with growing cross-platform engagement and a clear upward trend in audience responsiveness.

Website traffic in October reflected stable, high-quality engagement despite modest total user volume. Nearly 70 percent of sessions qualified as engaged, with users spending an average of almost two minutes per visit. Direct and organic search channels accounted for the majority of traffic, suggesting that Apollo continues to benefit from both strong brand visibility and search-driven discovery. It is worth noting that website analytics tracking was newly implemented this month, so these initial numbers will likely improve and better reflect true visitor volume and engagement trends as GA4 matures in November.

Recommendations & Next Steps

Strategic priorities for November 2025 and beyond

Social Media

Build on Momentum

  • Continue maintaining at least one post per week outside of Company news, aligned with the approved communications plan.
  • Prioritize content highlighting corporate and project milestones as financings, resource updates, and leadership commentary continue to drive the strongest engagement.
  • Begin cross-posting interviews, articles, and coverage of Ross and Andy to strengthen external visibility and cross-promotional reach.
  • Experiment with slightly higher posting cadence on LinkedIn (6 to 7 posts per month) to build on October's strong impression growth.

Mailouts

Optimize & Test

  • Maintain current frequency of timely, news-driven campaigns as open rates remain far above industry averages.
  • Test new subject line variations and preview text formats to further boost open and click-through performance.
  • Implement light A/B testing on subject line tone or CTA placement to identify which combinations best convert reader engagement into website traffic.

Website

Establish the Baseline

  • With GA4 now active, monitor data closely in November to establish a clearer performance baseline for visitor activity.
  • Optimize project and investor page SEO to strengthen organic search visibility, which already accounts for over 40 percent of total sessions.
  • Consider a visual refresh or additional highlight banners on the homepage to better direct new visitors toward investor materials and recent releases.

Ongoing

Track & Refine

  • Maintain this monthly reporting structure covering Social Media, Mailouts, Website, and Recommendations for ongoing performance benchmarking.
  • Track early GA4 trends in next month's report to confirm growth patterns and identify where audience quality and engagement improve most.
  • Explore integrating social referral tracking with UTM links in future mailouts to directly connect post engagement with site traffic and conversions.
